How It Works

Creating Ideabooks

  • Tap "New Ideabook" and describe what it's about
  • AI will transcribe and create a short, descriptive name
  • Choose from 6 beautiful colors to categorize your ideabook

Color System

Organize your ideabooks with our flexible color system:

Adding Ideas

  • Tap the microphone button on any ideabook
  • Record your idea (up to 1 minute)
  • AI transcribes, polishes, and adds emojis for delight
  • Ideas are automatically organized by date

AI Chat & Notes

  • Chat with AI about your ideabook contents
  • Get suggestions, expand ideas, or create stories
  • Save valuable AI responses as notes
  • Voice input supported in chat

Privacy & Security

  • Passcode Protection: Lock sensitive ideabooks with a secure passcode
  • Cloud Sync: Your data is securely stored and synced across devices
  • Authentication: Sign in with Google for secure access
  • Local Storage: Chat history stored locally for privacy

Tips for Best Experience

  • Speak clearly when recording voice memos
  • Use colors to categorize different types of ideas
  • Regularly chat with AI to develop your ideas further
  • Save important AI responses as notes for future reference
  • Use drag-and-drop to reorder ideas within ideabooks
